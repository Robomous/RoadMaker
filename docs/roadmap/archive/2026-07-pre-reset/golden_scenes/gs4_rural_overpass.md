# GS-4 — Rural overpass

*Golden scene for the Materials & Structures milestone (v0.7.0): a two-lane
rural road crossing another via a built **bridge structure**, exercising the
material system v2 (variants), the bridge generator, and concrete structure
material.*

Specified purely from ASAM OpenDRIVE concepts, per the
[product-parity rules](../../../../standards/product-parity.md).

## Scene definition

Two rural two-lane roads on different elevations, one bridging over the other:

- **Roads:** a lower road (straight `line` reference line, ~200 m) and an upper
  road crossing it at ~90°, carried over on a **bridge**. Each road: one
  `driving` lane per direction (3.5 m), a `shoulder` each side; the upper road's
  vertical profile lifts it clear of the lower road's envelope (grade in, level
  over the deck, grade out).
- **Bridge structure:** a **deck with visible thickness**, **abutments** at each
  bank, and **guardrails** on both sides of the upper road across the span —
  generated by the bridge tool over the grade separation (OpenDRIVE `<bridge>`
  records the span; the deck/abutment/guardrail solids are Manifold geometry).
  Under-deck clearance is visible from the fixed camera.
- **Materials:** **two distinct asphalt materials** (e.g. newer upper road vs.
  worn lower road) drawn from the material library, plus a **concrete structure
  material** on the deck/abutments — the milestone's material-variety proof.
- **Markings:** double-yellow center (`roadMark` `solid solid`, yellow) on both
  roads; broken white edge/lane lines as appropriate.
- **Props:** ~10 trees (`<object>` vegetation) scattered along the banks.
- **Ground:** grass ground at skirt level (M3a procedural ground / terrain
  skirt). Rolling hills are **not** in scope here — the M3b heightmap terrain
  ([ADR-0006](../../../../decisions/0006-terrain-scope.md)) brings sculpted ground
  later; GS-4 proves materials and structures on the flat skirt.
- **Lighting:** daytime sky/lighting (M3a), no shadow maps.

## Fixed camera

Kernel frame: right-handed, Z-up, meters. Crossing point (under the deck) near
the origin.

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Position | (−45.0, −40.0, 14.0) |
| Target | (0.0, 0.0, 2.0) |
| Up | +Z |
| Vertical FOV | 45° |
| Aspect | 16:9 (render at 1920×1080) |

A low three-quarter view looking toward the overpass — the deck reads against
the sky, the abutments and guardrails are legible, and the under-deck clearance
(the lower road passing beneath) is visible.

## Acceptance beyond the image

- The scene's `.xodr` validates with zero errors; new elements (`<bridge>`,
  materials) carry normative rule-id citations in any diagnostics
  ([references](../../../../domain/references.md)).
- Round-trip: write → parse → write is stable within `rm::tol` and loses no
  `<object>` / `<bridge>` / material data (per the material-persistence decision
  taken at planning time).
- Both asphalt variants and the concrete structure material are visibly distinct
  from the fixed camera.
- Scene loads and renders headless in the golden-screenshot CI workflow
  ([process](README.md)).
- Every asset used has its `ASSETS_LICENSES.md` row
  ([asset policy](../../../../standards/assets.md)).
