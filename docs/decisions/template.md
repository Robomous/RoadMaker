# ADR-NNNN: Short decision title

*Copy this file to `NNNN-short-slug.md` (next free number) and fill it in.
One decision per record.*

- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded by ADR-NNNN
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** maintainer name(s)

## Context

What situation forced a decision? Constraints, alternatives on the table,
and why the choice matters long-term. 2–4 paragraphs maximum.

## Decision

The decision, stated in one or two sentences, in the active voice
("We use X for Y").

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what follow-ups the decision
creates. Include the reversal cost — what it would take to change course.

## References

Links to design docs, spikes, issues, or external material that informed
the decision.
